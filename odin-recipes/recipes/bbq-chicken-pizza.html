BBQ Chicken Pizza

Description

A Popular, non-traditional pizza that replaces standard tomato marinara sauce with a sweet, tangy and smoky barbecue sauce with diced chicken, peppers, onion and cilantro all covered with cheese and backed to bubbly goodness.

Ingredients

  • Pre-baked pizza crust
  • Spicy barbecue sauce
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (cooked and cubed)
  • Sliced pepperoni peppers
  • Chopped red onion
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 °C
  2. Place pizza crust on baking sheet and spread barbecue sauce on top
  3. Top with pepperoni peppers, chicken, onion and cilantro
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly
