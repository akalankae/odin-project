Creamy Corn Soup

Description

A comforting golden-hued dish that balances the natural, sweet pop of sweetcorn with a rich, velvety base. Easy to make with canned corn, cream cheese, onions, garlic and parsley for a deliciously creamy, comforting soup.

Ingredients

  • ¹⁄₂ of chopped onion
  • ¹⁄₄ cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 2¹⁄₂ cup of milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 cans of drained kernel corn
  • 2¹⁄₂ tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Mix onion, parsley, butter, minced garlic in a pot
  2. Cook for 5 over medium heat
  3. Whisk in flour, milk and chicken broth until soup thickens
  4. Stir corn and cream cheese until heated through
  5. Add garlic salt, black pepper and cayenne pepper
  6. Stir until combined
