Lasagna

Description

One of the most beloved foods in the world. This is a traditional Italian Lasagna made with Bolognese ragu and cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
  • Pasta
  • Ricotta filling
  • Cheese topping

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Pour marinara sauce into meat mixture
  3. Make the pasta
  4. Make the filling
  5. Preheat oven to 190 °C
  6. Layer ingredients and layer them into the frying pan
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminium foil
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes
