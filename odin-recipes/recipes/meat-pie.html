Meat Pie

Description

Pie featuring beef, pork and potato with herbs and warm spices for a super-comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef and pork
  • Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Seasonings
  • Water
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 °C
  2. Boil potato with water for 5 minutes
  3. Drain, mash and set aside
  4. Crumble ground beef and pork into a large saucepan
  5. Add onion, garlic, mustard powder, thyme, sage, cloves, salt and black pepper and cook after stirring in water
  6. Remove from heat and mixed with mashed potato
  7. Ease one pie cruse into a plate, fill with meat mixture and top with remaining pie crust
  8. Trim excess dough and crimp the edges with tines of fork to seal
  9. Pick top crust few times to let out steam
